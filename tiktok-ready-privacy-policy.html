n8n TikTok Automation App - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

The n8n TikTok Automation App collects only the information necessary to provide the Service. This includes:

  • OAuth tokens for authenticating your TikTok account
  • Content metadata such as captions, hashtags, and scheduling information
  • Basic account identifiers from connected TikTok accounts

We do not collect passwords or unrelated personal information.

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used exclusively to authenticate your TikTok account, publish content on your behalf, and manage the workflow automation. We do not use your information for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data. Information is shared only with TikTok when necessary to perform posting actions through their API.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely for the minimal time necessary to provide the Service. We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of your information.

5. User Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your data. To do so, please contact us at alex@example.com. We will remove your information in compliance with your request within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of any modifications to this Policy.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns regarding your privacy or this Policy, please contact us at alex@example.com.
